2.7 · Consent & opt-in (GDPR)
  • Double opt-in for non-customer newsletter leads; store marketing_consent + timestamp + source.
  • Account-critical messages (failed payment, trial expiry) are transactional — they send regardless of marketing consent, but never carry promotional content.
  • Promotional and educational flows send only where marketing_consent = true.
  • Every marketing email includes one-click unsubscribe, the relevant suppression group and a postal address.
