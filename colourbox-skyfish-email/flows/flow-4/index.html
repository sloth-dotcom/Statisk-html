Flow 4 · Behavioural & Promotional

Real-time reactions to on-site behaviour, plus the planned promotional calendar. Highest revenue-per-email because they reach people at the exact moment of intent.

Primary KPI
Revenue per email
Benchmark
10–20% (abandonment)
Emails
7
Entry trigger
Real-time on-site behaviour + scheduled broadcasts.
Audience
All active accounts.
Cadence
7 emails + seasonal calendar.
Exit criteria
Download / licence event (or n/a for ongoing flows).
Delivery
Mostly event-triggered API sends.
Suppression group
New assets & inspiration, Offers & promotions.

4D · Post-download cross-sell

Increase assets per session and reinforce the download habit.

B
Colourbox/Skyfish <hello@mail.colourbox.com>
to {{first_name}} · All active accounts.
Email 4D.1
Subject
Nice pick — here’s what pairs well
Assets that work alongside your last download.
Hi {{first_name}}, great choice on your last download. Projects rarely need just one asset, so here’s a set that pairs naturally with it — same style, same shoot, or a matching series.
Trigger
A few hours after a download / licence event.
Audience
All active accounts.
Note: Branch on account_type: for credit buyers, add a soft line on bulk packs; for subscribers, nudge Collections.
