Activate

Flow 1 · Onboarding & Activation

Drive every new account to its first meaningful download (activation) and seed core habits: search, Collections, the DAM.

Primary KPI
Activation rate (first download)
Benchmark
35–50%
Emails
6
Entry trigger
account_created → contact enters the “New accounts” segment.
Audience
account_type = trial OR free.
Cadence
6 emails over ~9 days.
Exit criteria
subscription_started → exit; OR trial_end_date within 7 days → hand off to Flow 2A; OR series completes.
Delivery
Marketing Automation (segment-entry). Emails 2 and 6 gate on first_download_date being empty.
Suppression group
Product onboarding.
