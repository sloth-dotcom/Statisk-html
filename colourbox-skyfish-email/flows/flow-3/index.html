Reactivate

Flow 3 · Re-engagement & Win-back

Three sub-flows that catch customers at three different distances: the paying subscriber who has gone quiet, the customer who has churned, and the contact who no longer opens anything.

Primary KPI
Reactivation rate
Benchmark
10–20% / 5–12%
Emails
8
Entry trigger
Dormancy, cancellation, or 180 days without an open.
Audience
Subscribers, churned, unengaged.
Cadence
8 emails across 3 sub-flows.
Exit criteria
Login, repurchase, or any open / click.
Delivery
Marketing Automation + event-triggered API send.
Suppression group
New assets & inspiration, Offers & promotions.

Sub-flows

3A · Dormant subscriber rescue
3 emails

Most valuable re-engagement work: a paying-but-idle subscriber is a cancellation waiting to happen. Catch them before renewal.

3B · Lapsed / churned win-back
3 emails

A 45-day window while collections are still saved and the relationship is warm. Lead with what changed, then make the offer, then ask why.

3C · List hygiene & sunset
2 emails

Unglamorous, essential. Re-confirming or retiring contacts who never open protects the sender reputation every other flow depends on.

3C · List hygiene & sunset

Unglamorous, essential. Re-confirming or retiring contacts who never open protects the sender reputation every other flow depends on.

B
Colourbox/Skyfish <hello@mail.colourbox.com>
to {{first_name}} · Unengaged contacts.
Email 3C.1
Subject
Do you still want to hear from us?
We’ll only keep emailing if you say yes.
Hi {{first_name}}, we noticed you haven’t opened our emails in a while — no problem at all. If you’d still like Colourbox/Skyfish updates, just click below to stay subscribed. If not, you don’t need to do anything — we’ll stop, and your inbox stays clean.
Trigger
On entering the Unengaged segment.
Audience
Unengaged contacts.

Trigger

Audience
Still-unengaged contacts.
Note: Non-responders after this email: suppress from all marketing sends. Protects sender reputation.
